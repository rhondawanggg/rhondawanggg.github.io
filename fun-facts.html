Fun Facts 😆

Outside of crunching data and building models, I have a pretty fun side! Here are some random (and maybe a little weird) facts about me:

  • Hockey is my life 🏒 – If you ever hear me screaming at my screen, it’s probably because of a Northeastern Huskies game. LET’S GOOOOOO HUSKIES!!!🔥
  • Ramen addiction 🍜 – I could eat ramen every single day and still find an excuse to try another flavor. Spicy miso? Yes, please. Extra toppings? Absolutely.
  • My playlist is chaotic 🎶 – One moment, I’m listening to chill indie music, the next I’m jamming to K-pop or 2000s throwbacks. My Spotify algorithm is so confused.
  • If I could time travel, I’d go to the future first 🚀 – Just to see if we finally have flying cars and AI that does my homework for me.
  • MBTI: ISFP 🎨 – Aka, the artistic introvert. I love designing, painting, and occasionally getting lost in Pinterest boards I’ll never actually recreate.
  • Coffee or tea? ☕ – Both, but if you hand me a matcha latte, I will be your best friend.
  • Spontaneous travel? Sign me up! 🛫 – I have a bad habit of planning trips on impulse… and somehow making them work.
  • Dog lover 🐶 – I adore dogs and will 100% stop mid-conversation to pet one. Big or small, they all deserve love.

If any of these facts made you laugh, we’d probably get along. If not... well, at least you know I really love ramen. 🍜
