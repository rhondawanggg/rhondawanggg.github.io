About Me 👩🏻‍💻

Hi, I'm Rhonda Wang! 💗 If I had to describe myself in one sentence, I'd say I'm someone who loves asking questions, exploring patterns, and solving problems using data. Data science fascinates me because it's like putting together pieces of a puzzle—except the puzzle is the real world, and the answers can actually make a difference.

I didn't set out to become a data scientist, but I got really into it when I stumbled across it. I've always been curious and enjoy understanding how things work. I thought I'd go down the more traditional tech route, but as soon as I started working with Python, data visualization, and machine learning models, I knew I'd found something special. There's something really exciting about taking raw data—just numbers and text—and turning it into something meaningful.

I’m currently exploring machine learning, predictive analytics, and data storytelling. I love projects where I can take messy, complex datasets and uncover insights that help people make better decisions. Whether it's analyzing crime trends, optimizing business operations, or even just figuring out why certain songs go viral on Spotify, I’m always eager to dive into a challenge.

This website is a collection of my projects, interests, and thoughts. If you’re curious about what I’ve been working on, check out my Projects page. Want to know some random facts about me? Head over to Fun Facts.
