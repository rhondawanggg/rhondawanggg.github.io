About Me

Hi, I'm Rhonda! If I had to describe myself in one sentence, I’d say I’m someone who loves asking questions, exploring patterns, and solving problems using data. Data science fascinates me because it’s like putting together pieces of a puzzle—except the puzzle is the real world, and the answers can actually make a difference.

My journey into data science wasn’t entirely planned, but rather something I stumbled upon and fell in love with. I’ve always been a naturally curious person, drawn to understanding why things work the way they do. Initially, I thought I would pursue a more traditional path in tech, but the moment I started working with Python, data visualization, and machine learning models, I knew I had found something special. There’s something incredibly exciting about taking raw data—just numbers and text—and transforming it into a meaningful story.

I’m currently exploring machine learning, predictive analytics, and data storytelling. I love projects where I can take messy, complex datasets and uncover insights that help people make better decisions. Whether it's analyzing crime trends, optimizing business operations, or even just figuring out why certain songs go viral on Spotify, I’m always eager to dive into a challenge.

This website is a collection of my projects, interests, and thoughts. If you’re curious about what I’ve been working on, check out my Projects page. Want to know some random facts about me? Head over to Fun Facts.
